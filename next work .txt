One architectural recommendation

Since I know from our previous discussions that your long-term goal is a multi-tenant SaaS e-commerce platform, I would make one major architectural change before the project grows further:

Instead of building NOX as a single-store application first, design it from the start with a tenant_id (or store_id) across tenant-owned models and strict data isolation. That will make it much easier to evolve into a Shopify-like SaaS later without a painful database migration.

With that change and the missing enterprise features added, NOX could realistically become a strong, production-grade SaaS platform rather than just an online store.
